OVERVIEW

WHAT IS SYNERGYCON

SynergyCon is designed to foster collaboration between Nigeria’s creative, digital, and tech sectors. It’s a platform where innovative projects and startups at the intersection of creativity and technology are showcased, driving progress and pushing boundaries. At SynergyCon, professionals and investors come together to network, exchange ideas, and explore new opportunities. Through workshops, conversations, and panel discussions, we aim to inspire the next generation of creatives and tech innovators, empowering them to shape the future.

Join us at SynergyCon—where creativity meets technology, and the future begins!
